Frequently
Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, stylists, and hair care.

Do I need to book in advance? +
We recommend booking at least a day in advance to secure your preferred stylist and time.
What products do you use? +
We use top-tier brands like L'Oréal, Olaplex, and Kerastase for optimal results.
Can I bring my own hair extensions? +
Yes, we're happy to install client-provided extensions upon consultation.
What if I'm not satisfied with my service? +
Client satisfaction is our priority. Please contact us within 48 hours and we'll make it right.
Is my consultation information kept confidential? +
Absolutely. We maintain strict confidentiality of all client information and consultation details.
